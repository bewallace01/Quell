## Description (4000 characters max)

```
quell is for the moments before the urge wins.

no fixing. no tracking. no restriction. just presence.

when the wave is rising, you tap "in it" and quell is there:
a slow breath, a quiet voice, the smallest amount of structure
to keep you company while the wave passes — or while you eat
anyway, with someone present.

— what's inside —

co-regulation. a soft breathing field with a voice that doesn't
push, doesn't lecture, doesn't rush. just sits.

the fork. when you're ready to do something, three soft choices
— body, mood, don't know — leading to short, real protocols
matched to what you're actually feeling.

eat anyway. the philosophical centerpiece. when you're going
to eat, quell stays with you. mindful or just-eat. no food
details captured. ever.

sensory swaps. when your body wants crunch, cold, warmth,
sweet, or salty — concrete suggestions, food and non-food.

future-you. record up to five short voice notes from your
sober self. they surface gently during co-regulation.

your patterns. a quiet calendar that celebrates showing up.
no streaks, no numbers, no shaming.

stealth. shake your phone and the screen instantly disguises
as a notes app. for moments when shame walks into the room.

— what's not inside —

no calorie tracking. no weight tracking. no food log. no
"healthy" / "unhealthy" framing. no daily streaks. no
gamification. no social feed. no ads. no analytics. no
account.

— privacy —

everything you save stays on your device. no cloud sync,
no tracking, no third-party sharing.

— a note on professional care —

quell is a supplement to professional care, not a replacement.
988 (suicide & crisis lifeline) is always one tap away.
if you're struggling, please also reach out to a clinician.
```

*(approx. 1700 chars — well under the 4000 limit, leaving room for clinical advisor credit and additional features in updates)*

## App Review Information

### Notes to App Review

Apple's review for ED-adjacent apps is careful. Include in the notes:

```
quell is a co-regulation app for moments of binge-eating urges.
it is explicitly anti-diet, anti-restriction, and anti-tracking.
no calories, no weight, no food logs.

the app frames eating itself as neutral data, not failure
("eat anyway" mode supports the user during eating without
judgment). all content is supportive, never restrictive.

crisis resources (988, 741741, 911) are accessible within 2 taps
```
