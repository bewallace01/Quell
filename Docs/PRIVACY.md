# Quell — Privacy Policy

> Draft. Review for accuracy before publishing. Apple App Review and the App Store listing both require a public Privacy Policy URL.

## tl;dr

Quell does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers. Everything you save lives on your device, in your app's local storage, and nowhere else.

## What we collect

**Nothing leaves your device.** Specifically:

- **Voice notes you record** are stored as audio files in your app's local Documents directory and metadata in iOS UserDefaults. They never leave your device. We have no servers.
- **Engagement events** (timestamps when you complete urge-flow sessions) are stored locally in UserDefaults to power the in-app "your patterns" view. They are never transmitted off-device.
- **Settings preferences** (haptic on/off, audio on/off, onboarding completion flag) are stored in iOS UserDefaults on your device.

We do not collect: your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, food logs, weight, calories, biometric data, IP address, advertising ID, or any other personal information.

## What we do not do

- **No analytics.** No third-party SDKs, no event tracking, no crash reporting services. No Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Firebase Analytics, no Apple Analytics.
- **No advertising.** No ad networks, no IDFA, no tracking.
- **No cloud sync.** Your data does not back up to iCloud (unless you have iOS automatic app-data backup enabled, which is your choice and managed by Apple).
- **No accounts.** We have no login system, no email collection, no password — there is nothing to sign up for.
- **No third-party data sharing.** We have nothing to share, since we collect nothing.

## iOS permissions we request

We request these permissions only at the moment you actually use the related feature, never at launch:

- **Microphone** — only when you tap "record" to make a voice note. Recordings stay on your device. We do not transmit audio anywhere.
- **Notifications** — only when you start the "just eat, i'll check back" flow. We schedule a single local notification that fires after 20 minutes ("checking back. still here when you want me.") to gently return you to the app. We do not send push notifications from a server.

You can revoke either permission at any time in iOS Settings.

## Crisis resource numbers

The app provides tap-to-call shortcuts for 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), 741741 (Crisis Text Line), and 911. These are direct iOS phone/SMS handoffs. We do not log, observe, or transmit anything about whether you tap these numbers.

## Children's privacy

Quell is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted here and dated. Material changes (e.g., if we ever introduce optional cloud sync or analytics — which would require updating the in-app About copy first) will be communicated via the app.
